PRECAUTION MEASURES

Coronavirus precaution

5 ways to make sure you do not catch the disease 

Hand Wash and Sanitization

Wash your hands frequently,
Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based sanetizer or wash them with soap

Practise respiratory hygiene

This means covering your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze.Then dispose the tissue immediately.

Avoid touching face

Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth
Our hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses.Once contaminated,hands can transfer the virus to your eyes,nose or mouth.

SOCIAL DISTANCING

Maintain Social distancing
Maintain at least 2 metre (6 feet)
distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

Dont be Doctor yourself!

If you have fever, cough and difficulty in breathing, seek medical care only.
